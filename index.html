The New Planning Experience

Smart To-Do List

Entering tons of data can be very annoying. With FullUse, enter minimal data and receive a to-do list with the organization standards of other high-end apps!

It can be inconvenient for users to enter lots of data in productivity applications. With FullUse, enter minimal data and categorize your tasks into Short Term Tasks and Long Term Tasks.

Tasks

There are 2 types of tasks. The Short Term Tasks, and the Long Term Tasks. Short Term Tasks are one-off things. Long Term Tasks span over a duration.

Priority Sort

Set a Task Priority for your tasks and watch as your to do list is automagically sorted.

Notifications

FullUse automatically notifies you 2 days before your time is up. Custom reminders are overrated.

Editing

Made a mistake? Fret not! Our Tasks are completely editable!

Your Very Own Tree

Grow your very own tree by completing tasks. Inculcate discipline, responsibility, and raise awareness for the environment.

Growth & Water

Complete tasks to grow your plant from a little seed to a tree. Stay committed and maintain its hydration level.

Stages

The plant has 4 stages: Seed → Shoot → Sapling → Full Tree. Each stage has 5 hydration states, depending on the water level.
